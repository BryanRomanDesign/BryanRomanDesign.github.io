Pawlower


Pawlower is a clothing brand dedicated to promoting mental health through the bond between humans and pets. The project involved developing a strategic brand identity, including a cohesive visual system, logo design, and a clothing collection that embodies the brand’s mission. Through thoughtful design and storytelling, Pawlower merges fashion with purpose, encouraging conversations around mental well-being.

Brand Purpose:

Pawlower is a clothing brand that merges fashion with purpose, using design to spark conversations about mental health. By highlighting the emotional support that pets provide, the brand aims to promote self-care, resilience, and well-being in everyday life.



Target Audience:

Pawlower is designed with a focus for young adults and adults, a demographic most prone to experiencing mental health challenges due to stress, societal pressures, and life transitions. By integrating meaningful design with a message of emotional support, the brand resonates with individuals seeking comfort, self-expression, and a deeper connection with their pets.



Core Values:

  • Well-being: Promoting self-care and mental health awareness.
  • Compassion: Strengthening the bond between humans and pets for emotional support.
  • Pet-Centered: Celebrating the bond between pets and owners in promoting well-being.
  • Trustworthy: Building a brand based on authenticity and positive impact.

Pawlower's inspiration comes from my personal journey with Tako, my dog. Tako has taught me invaluable lessons from unconditional love and the strength of friendship to the responsibility of caring for another life. He has been a constant source of support, helping me navigate stress, anxiety, and loneliness. No matter what happens, no matter who I am or what I go through, Tako is always there for me, unwavering and trustworthy.

The meaning of Pawlower's name.


The name "Pawlower" is a fusion of two words: "Paw" and "Follower." Together, they form "Pawlower," representing the unique bond between animals and humans. This bond, often unspoken, subtly guides our lives in ways we may not always realize. Pawlower symbolizes how pets are always by our side, leading us through a journey filled with emotions, joy, and unconditional support.

Pawlower’s imagery centers around the positive and uplifting connection between pets and their owners. The visuals often feature dogs in natural, joyful settings, capturing moments of calm, comfort, and companionship. The use of soft, calming colors like pastels and earth tones reflects the brand’s focus on mental well-being and tranquility. The overall aesthetic is warm, inviting, and emotional, evoking feelings of happiness, peace, and unconditional love.

When designing merchandise for Pawlower, I explored a variety of concepts across different styles. Some designs had a more friendly, approachable feel, while others were more playful and "childish." Many of these concepts didn't make it into the first collection, as they didn’t align with the brand’s desired tone, particularly for the debut release. However, this doesn’t rule out the possibility of these designs being released in the future as side products in the shop.

'A New Beginning' marks the debut collection of Pawlower, setting the tone for the brand’s mission to promote mental health through the bond with pets. This collection highlights the various ways pets can support us in moments of stress, anxiety, or loneliness. The designs playfully capture these simple yet powerful actions.

Pawlower’s mission is built on a solid foundation of compassion, mental health awareness, and the unbreakable bond between humans and pets. We strive to create a world where mental health is openly supported, and no one has to suffer in silence. Through our brand, we hope to raise awareness and encourage adoption, ensuring that every pet finds a loving home while offering their unconditional support. Pawlower is a strong, unique brand with endless potential, and we are committed to making a positive impact: one design, one pet, and one person at a time. Together, we can create a future where both people and pets thrive.
