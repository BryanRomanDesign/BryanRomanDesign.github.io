General Questions:

Why is branding important?

+

Branding helps your business stand out, connect with the right audience, and build trust. It’s more than just a logo—it’s the way your business is perceived.

I’ve worked with a designer before and it didn’t go well—how are you different?

+

I’m sorry to hear about your past experience. I prioritize creating a comfortable and collaborative process. My approach is friendly and informal, like chatting with a trusted friend. This helps us stay aligned on your brand’s vision while building trust. I’m here to design a brand that resonates with your ideal clients and supports your business goals.

I’m just starting out—is now the right time to invest in branding?

+

The best time to invest in branding is when you’re ready to elevate your business. I offer flexible packages tailored to businesses at any stage, including custom options to fit your needs. If you’re unsure, reach out via email, and I’ll walk you through the process and how branding can set your business on the right path.

Can I just get a logo?

+

My packages focus on creating a cohesive brand identity, not just a standalone logo. The basic visual identity package includes a logo, color palette, and typography pairings to establish a strong foundation for your business. As your brand grows, I offer discounted upgrades to expand your identity. Contact me to discuss your specific needs!

How long does the branding process take?

+

The timeline depends on the package and complexity of your project, but most branding projects take 4–8 weeks. This includes discovery, design, and revisions. I’ll provide a detailed timeline when we start, and we’ll work together to meet your schedule. Let me know if you have a specific deadline!

What types of businesses do you work with?

+

I work with businesses of all sizes and industries, from startups to established brands, whether you’re in retail, tech, creative services, or something unique. My process is tailored to fit your specific goals and audience. Curious if we’re a good match? Drop me a message, and let’s chat!

Workflow & Communication:

How do you handle communication during the branding process?

+

I prefer written communication, such as email or messaging, as it’s comfortable for me and accessible for everyone. It allows you to reach out anytime, and I’ll respond promptly when available. Written communication also helps us keep a clear record of discussions, reducing misunderstandings.

How do you ensure the design reflects my brand?

+

I start by researching your industry, understanding your brand’s goals, vision, and values. This ensures the design aligns with your purpose and resonates with your audience. Through collaborative feedback, we refine the design to feel authentic to your brand.

Will I communicate with you directly?

+

Yes, you’ll work directly with me! You can choose your preferred channel—email, social media (like Instagram or Facebook), or a Notion portal. I recommend social media for casual chats to keep things friendly and approachable.

Can I give feedback if I’m not experienced with design?

+

Absolutely! Your feedback is invaluable, no design expertise needed. I’ll guide you through the process, asking for your thoughts at key stages. We’ll discuss what works and why, ensuring your brand feels right for you and your audience.

What makes good feedback?

+

Good feedback is honest and specific—tell me what you like, dislike, or want to tweak. You don’t need to give feedback just to contribute; focus on what feels right for your brand. I’ll balance your input with my expertise to find the best path forward.

What’s your process for designing a brand identity?

+

My process includes five phases: Discovery, Research, Design, Refinement, and Delivery. Each phase is tailored to your project, and I’ll provide a detailed breakdown when we start. This structured approach ensures your brand is strategic and cohesive.

How do you handle tight deadlines or urgent projects?

+

If you have a tight deadline, let me know upfront, and I’ll do my best to accommodate it without compromising quality. Urgent projects may involve a faster-paced process or prioritized scheduling, and I’ll keep you updated every step of the way. Reach out to discuss your timeline!

Revisions:

What counts as a revision?

+

Minor changes that align with your brand’s goals typically don’t count as revisions. However, multiple small tweaks or significant changes, like altering the design direction, will count. I’ll let you know before performing any revision if it counts toward your limit.

Can I get more revisions if I run out?

+

If you need additional revisions after using your allocated ones, I can offer more for a small fee. We’ll discuss the cost upfront before proceeding.

Deliverables & Files:

What files are included in my package?

+

All packages include the following file formats: PNG, SVG, JPG, and AI (Adobe Illustrator) containing the final designs.

Will I get the source files used during the project?

+

You’ll receive an Adobe Illustrator (AI) file with all final designs, but not the working files containing drafts or previous versions. This ensures you have everything needed for your brand while keeping the design process streamlined.

Can I update my brand assets later?

+

After paying the final invoice, you can modify your assets, but I don’t recommend it without professional guidance, as it may weaken your brand identity. For brands I’ve designed, I offer discounted services for updates or additional assets. Contact me for support!

Payment:

How is payment handled?

+

Payments are typically split into two invoices: one at the start of the project and one before final file delivery. The first payment is required to begin, and the final payment releases your deliverables.

Can invoices be split into smaller payments?

+

For larger projects, I can split invoices into smaller payments (e.g., four or six installments) if needed. Let me know before the first invoice, and we’ll find a payment plan that works for both of us.

Can I get a refund?

+

Refunds are not available after invoices are paid, as you’re paying for a custom service, not a product.

What if I want to cancel the project or don’t like the design after using all revisions?

+

If you’re unsatisfied after using all revisions, you can reject the design. This means you cannot use any concepts, drafts, or final designs. If you cancel, you won’t be charged the final invoice.

Can I pause the project?

+

I can pause a project for up to 3 days per feedback request to give you time to review. If more time is needed, please notify me. After 5 days without communication, the project may be considered abandoned, requiring a fee to resume or restart. Let’s stay in touch to avoid this!

What payment methods do you accept?

+

I accept Wire Transfer, PayPal, and ATH Móvil (Puerto Rico only). Invoices include all payment details for a smooth process.

What happens if I don’t pay an invoice on time?

+

Late payments may incur interest as outlined in the invoice terms and conditions. Please review these details and reach out if you anticipate any issues.

Do you offer discounts for returning clients or multiple projects?

+

I love working with clients again! Returning clients or those with multiple projects may be eligible for special rates or package deals. Let’s discuss your needs, and I’ll see how I can make it work for you.
