Ember & Pine


Ember & Pine is a candle brand inspired by the grounding scents of the outdoors, bringing the warmth of a flickering flame and the freshness of the forest into your space.

Ember & Pine’s visual identity captures the essence of nature’s tranquility, creating a brand that feels like a cozy escape into the outdoors. The design invites customers to relax and reconnect with the earth, making each candle more than just a product, but an experience.

Approach: The branding for Ember & Pine blends rustic charm with modern elegance, drawing inspiration from the natural world. The design uses earthy tones, organic textures, and simple, timeless typography to evoke warmth, calm, and connection to nature.



Concept: The logo features a combination of tree symbols, minimalistic flame details, and a rustic yet modern font to symbolize both the natural and comforting qualities of the product. The packaging and visual elements reflect the serene, earthy tones of a forest, creating an inviting atmosphere. This balance between nature-inspired design and warmth conveys a sense of trust, relaxation, and authenticity, making the brand approachable while standing out on the shelf.
