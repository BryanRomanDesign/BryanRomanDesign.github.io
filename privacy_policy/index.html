Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

Bryan Román Design (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website bryanroman.design and interact with our services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information You Provide: When you fill out contact forms on our site, we collect information such as your first name, last name, business name, budget, project description, and email address.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about your interactions with our website, including pages visited and actions taken.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and gather analytics data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide our branding services.
  • To improve and personalize your experience on our website.
  • To analyze website traffic and ad campaign effectiveness using tools like the Meta (Facebook) Pixel.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Meta (Facebook) Pixel

We use the Meta Pixel on our website to better understand how visitors interact with our site and improve our marketing efforts. This may result in you seeing ads from us on Facebook or Instagram based on your activity.

Meta may use cookies and other technologies to collect or receive information from our website and elsewhere on the internet to provide measurement services and targeted advertising. You can manage your ad preferences and opt-out of personalized ads at Meta’s ad settings.

Cookies for Language Preference and Website Functionality

Our website uses cookies to remember your preferred language settings and provide a better browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help the website remember information about your visit.

These cookies do not collect personal information about you. They only store basic preferences such as your chosen language so you don’t need to select it every time you visit.

Additionally, cookies related to the Meta Pixel help us measure and improve the effectiveness of our ads by anonymously tracking user behavior on our site.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings; however, disabling some cookies may affect the functionality and experience of our website.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our website and run marketing campaigns, such as Meta Platforms, under strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Choices and Rights

You may contact us anytime to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.

Please contact us at: contact@bryanroman.design for any privacy-related requests.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
