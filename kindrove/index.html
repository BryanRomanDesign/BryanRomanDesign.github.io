Kindrove


Kindrove is a daycare brand inspired by the world of dinosaurs, designed to spark curiosity, adventure, and discovery among children. The brand aims to create a fun, educational, and safe environment where kids can learn, play, and explore.

Brand Purpose:

To inspire young explorers by providing a nurturing space where curiosity and imagination can thrive, grounded in a theme of adventure and discovery.



Target Audience:

Parents of children aged 0-12 years looking for a fun, safe, and educational daycare experience.



Core Values:

  • Curiosity: Encouraging children to ask questions and explore their surroundings.
  • Adventure: Creating an exciting and playful environment where children can experience new things.
  • Learning: Fostering a love of knowledge through age-appropriate activities and lessons.
  • Safety: Ensuring a secure and caring space for all children.

The Kindrove logo is a playful and dynamic representation that aligns with the brand’s theme of dinosaurs, exploration, and discovery. The logo’s dinosaur mascot helps children easily identify with the daycare’s divisions, while the name ‘Kindrove’ captures a sense of kindness and growth.

The color palette uses bright diverse colors to communicate a friendly, diverse and fun brand. The brand also uses illustrations of dinosaurs to create a consistent theme across the whole identity. This also helps the brand attract parents because kids just love dinosaurs. It encourages them to explore and learn about these magnificent creatures that roamed earth once.

Kindrove is more than just a daycare—it is a place of adventure, exploration, and discovery. Through its dinosaur-themed environment, fun activities, and nurturing approach, Kindrove aims to ignite a love of learning and create unforgettable memories for children.
