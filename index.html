• Who am I? •

HEY, I'M BRYAN!

I’m a graphic designer with a Master’s Degree in Graphic Arts, dedicated to solving complex design challenges and bringing impactful ideas to life. My expertise lies at the intersection of brand identity and digital interaction. I love the intricate process of shaping a brand's narrative from scratch, but I am equally passionate about building the digital environments where those brands live. By bridging the gap between high-end design and functional web structures, I ensure every project is purposeful, visually striking, and rooted in solid design principles.

• Why Me? •

Crafting Meaningful Experiences. Designed with Purpose.

To me, a purposeful experience means skipping the rigid, off-the-shelf templates to build custom digital solutions tailored entirely to your business goals. I believe a great website can only exist when it's built on a strong, intentional visual foundation. Whether we are launching a brand from scratch, refining an existing one, or translating your current identity into the digital space, I connect meaningful design with modern web structures. By balancing familiar user habits with high-end, custom execution from the first layout to the final digital interaction, I ensure your business looks premium, stays consistent, and feels effortless for your audience to navigate.

PAWFECT DAYS

Pawfect Days is a premium dog daycare brand identity that balances a joyful, energetic aesthetic with professional credibility. Built around a clean vector mascot and organic color palette, the system ensures a welcoming, premium presence across both web layouts and physical touchpoints.

PEGASUS

Pegasus Mobile Detailing is a brand refresh and website redesign developed to pivot an established car detailing business into the premium aircraft market. The project elevates the visual identity to attract a high-end audience while ensuring a consistent, premium digital presence across a completely restructured Wix website.

BRANDING

STRATEGY

HAPPY PAWS

Happy Paws is a visual brand identity designed to bridge the gap between high-end professional grooming and a playful, high-energy personality. Built around a stylish poodle mascot and a memorable visual system, the brand communicates trust and meticulous attention to detail while ensuring a warm, approachable, and premium digital presence that stands out to discerning pet owners.

PRIMAL BURGER

Primal Burger is a bold brand identity designed to embrace a raw, back-to-basics philosophy rooted in the primal joy of open-fire cooking. The visual system uses an unapologetic, authentic aesthetic to reflect strength and hearty portions, creating a memorable experience that elevates a simple culinary concept into a powerful, premium brand presence.
